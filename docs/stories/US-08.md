# US-08: Trading Session - Finalize

**Priority:** High (MVP Core)

**As a** User,
**I want** to confirm the trade and automatically update my collection,
**So that** I don't have to manually edit my binder after a trade.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **AC1:** Both users must click "Confirm".
- [ ] **AC2:** On success, traded cards are removed from the respective binders.
- [ ] **AC3:** Received cards are added to the respective binders.
